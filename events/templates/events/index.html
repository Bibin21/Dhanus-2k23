Every great institution has had a humble beginning, and College of Engineering, Kallooppara is no exception. Established in 1999 with a humble, but great goal of empowering the students from lesser backgrounds to achieve the pinnacle of success through the field of Engineering, our college has succeeded in various fields gaining district and state-level recognition for its achievements. As a part of this goal, our college is proud to present 'DHANUS 2023', the annual Tech Fest taking place in the college campus on ?/4/2023. This event provides a platform for students to showcase their prowess in the field of engineering aswell as highlight their creativity which is emancipated by their unique perspective of viewing the world through the eye of the common man which provides a level of versatility that assures an amazing experience. With various technical aswell as entertainment events catered to the audience, we welcome you to this event and thank you for giving us the opportunity to showcase our talents.
